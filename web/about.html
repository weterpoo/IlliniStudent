About iS

All of UIUC websites combined into one!

Remember working on that assignment on Compass2g?
Then you need to check your homework on Moodle,
but since they are independent websites,
you need to go to Moodle on a separate tab, and
then log in to check the homework. In comes illiniStudent,
a student made website aimed to ease the lives of
each student through integration of the UIUC websites
and an interactive calendar to remind you of upcoming
homework that is due.
