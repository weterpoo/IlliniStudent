About illiniStudent

All of UIUC websites organized and made simple for you!


What is illiniStudent?

          Remember working on that assignment on Compass2g? Then you realize you need to check your homework on Moodle but since they are independent websites, you need to go to Moodle on a separate tab, and then log in to check the homework. In comes illiniStudent, a student made website aimed to ease the lives of students through one simple login of NetID and Password to access an integration of the UIUC websites and an interactive calendar to remind you of upcoming homework that is due.


What can I do on this website?

          Currently the website is under construction. However, when the website is finished, it is expected to have the following functions:

  • Fully functioning account system allowing for personalized tasks
  • A hub for all the UIUC websites so you can easily access them while staying on the same page
  • Calendar to help organize tasks
  • And much more!
